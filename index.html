About

Western District Convention is the culminating event of the year for the West. It's a fun, educational (albeit sleepless) weekend of business and celebration. There will be workshops, panels, a chapter meet and greet, ritual, reading band, and banquet and dance. The new District Officers will be elected, the 2017 WDC host will be chosen, committees will discuss various topics to help make the District even stronger, and while all of this is happening you can meet new Brothers and Sisters from other chapters. Check out the schedule for more details about the weekend!

This year, WDC comes to dark and rainy Seattle, or rather the Southcenter DoubleTree Suites Hotel in Tukwila, WA. Gamma of UW will be hosting convention this spring! We're thrilled to welcome you to this landmark city on the Washington coast. This year's theme is "Served Hot or Iced", so come get swept up by the coffee culture while getting down to business with your Brothers and Sisters.

Convention will be held at the near the Seatac airport from April 8-10, 2016. When else will you be able to elect a District Council, grab some Starbucks, and dash off to the Space Needle on your way home? Register now to take part in an awesome WDC Seattle!

FAQ

When can I register?

Registration is open now! Normal registration goes until March 25th. Late registration is open until April 7. (Late registration is $100)

How much does WDC cost?

The registration fee is $72, and late registration after March 25th is $100. T-shirts cost $10 and you can pay for those when you register. Some will also be available for purchase at WDC, while supplies last. Hotel rooms are $109 per night. WDC 2016 lasts two nights, but usually you'll split room costs among the 4 people staying in the room.

How do I register for WDC 2016?
You'll need to pay the registration fee through the link to the Square marketplace, then fill out the registration form with your information. Go to the registration section of the website for a step by step explanation of this process.

Are there WDC t-shirts?

Yes!! There will be a WDC 2016 event t-shirt. Design previews are coming soon, but the shirt will cost $10. To pay for the t-shirt, select the option “WDC 2016 registration + t-shirt” on the square marketplace, and select your size on the registration form. T-shirts will also be sold at WDC weekend too!

What does the WDC hotel offer and how can I book a hotel room?

The hotel offers:

  1. *FREE Parking for day and overnight parking!
  2. *complimentary shuttle transportation to/from airport
  3. *Complimentary use of fitness center & business center
  4. *Free shuttle within 1 mile radius of hotel
  5. *Free chocolate chip cookies upon check-in
  6. *Free internet if HHonors member – (Is no cost to sign-up! – so sign-up for your free guest room internet during the conference!)

You can book your hotel either by phone or online, using the group name “Kappa Kappa Psi/Tau Beta Sigma” and group code “KKY”. See the instructions under the Registration section and the hotel booking document for more information.

What's happening at WDC 2016?

A whole bunch of things! There are workshops, Kappa Kappa Psi and Tau Beta Sigma ritual ceremonies, a banquet, a reading band, and much more!

What's there to do in Seattle?

Besides drink coffee? Plenty! The Southcenter area has an entire mall to explore, just blocks from the hotel. Downtown Seattle has all kinds of sights to see- the Space Needle, Pike Place Market, the Seattle Art Museum, and much much more. If you come a few days beforehand or stay a few days afterward, there's a ton of cool stuff you can experience. See the Attractions section for a more detailed description of everything you can see and do.

I still have questions that weren't answered here. What should I do next?

Ask us! The WDC Chairs, Matthew Braun and Sam Svilar, can be reached at kkygammawdc@gmail.com. They are happy to answer any and all questions you might still have!
